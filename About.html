ABOUT


Fresh-Pak Company is into manufacturing of food pakaging products. We uses General Purpose polystyrene for our production purposes. Fresh-Pak Company was establish in the year 2014. We have been in business for nine years providing products all over the markets and that is because of our quality products and the trust the market has in us.


LOCATION


Fresh-Pak Company is a ghana base company. It is located in the Central Region, on the CapeCoast road at the GN Industrial Estate, Essaman. Elmina-Ghana.


PRODUCTS


We produce disposable food packages for both Ghana and the African market. Our products ranges from Taskeaway packs, disposable cups, plastic spoons and forks, special trays, compartment packs and cutleries of higher quality for all occasions.

ABOUT US

Fresh-Pak is a producer of packages for all food items. it is a market-oriented company and so quality touches to the heart of its operational process.
